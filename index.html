Chicken noodle stirfry

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 3
Ingredients Quantity
Noodles 500g
Green Onion 20g
Boneless Chicken 250g
Soy Sauce 1/2 cup

Step 1: Boil noodles
Step 2: Strain noodles
Step 3: Fry 1/2 of green onions
Step 4: Add chicken, cook until fully cooked
Step 5: Season with salt, pepper, chili flakes, sesame seeds
Step 6: Add noodles, mix in soy sauce, serve hot

Hot veggie soup

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Water 8 Cups
Green Onion 20g
Carrots 50g
Broccoli 50g

Step 1: Boil veggies
Step 2: Add salt and pepper, put lid on top of pot and let it boil for 5 minutes
Step 3: Add hot sauce, serve hot

Grilled Cheese

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Cheese 1 slice
Bread 2 slices
Butter 1 spoon

Step 1: Add butter to pan, toast bread
Step 2: Add cheese to both pieces of bread
Step 3: add cover to melt cheese
Step 4: stick two pieces together
Step 5: Season with salt, pepper, chili flakes, sesame seeds
Step 6: (Optional) Serve with tomato soup
